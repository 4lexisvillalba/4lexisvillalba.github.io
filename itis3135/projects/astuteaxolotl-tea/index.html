Regulative Tea Blends

Astute Axolotl crafts small-batch regulative teas designed to help your body and mind find their natural pace again. Each blend is inspired by the axolotl’s steady, restorative nature—gentle, resilient, and a little bit luminous.

We pair organic leaves, florals, and botanicals with carefully tuned flavor profiles: nothing harsh, nothing rushed—just slow-steeping comfort brewed for nervous system softening, gut balance, and end-of-day unwinding.

  • Lakebed Lavender Chai — grounding spices with lavender to ease evening spirals.
  • Glow Mint Citrus — bright mint and citrus peel to regulate focus without the jitters.
  • Regenera Rose Oolong — floral, slightly creamy, brewed for calm digestion.
  • Deep Drift Chamomile — chamomile, skullcap, and lemon balm for soft sleep landings.
  • Brine & Bloom Green — coastal mineral notes and green tea for daytime clarity.
  • Club Night Decaf Noir — smoky, caffeine-free blend for late conversations and slow playlists.
