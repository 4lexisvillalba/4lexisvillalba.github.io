Rooted in Charlotte • Led by Rutilo De La Luz

Handy Work, Born to Serve

T&O Construction grew from Rutilo’s drive to combine precise craftsmanship with care for his community. What started as small repairs for friends has expanded into full exterior and interior renovation services trusted throughout the Queen City.

Our Story

After apprenticing under seasoned roofers and remodelers, Rutilo founded T&O to provide a dependable option for homeowners in Charlotte’s diverse neighborhoods. His goal was simple: show up prepared, communicate clearly, and leave every property better than it was found. That promise still guides the company today, whether we are repairing a roofline after summer storms or planning a full exterior redesign.

The “T&O” name is a tribute to his family, reminding the crew that every project protects a real household. Each estimate, material selection, and clean-up checklist reflects that responsibility.

Mission & Values

We exist to reinforce homes the right way—combining quality products, transparent communication, and a service mindset that puts people first. Rutilo instills these values across the team so every specialist approaches their craft with pride and humility.

  • Craftsmanship: Roofing, siding, exterior and interior remodeling done with certified processes.
  • Honesty: Straightforward scopes of work, clear pricing, and proactive updates.
  • Community focus: Supporting Charlotte families, neighborhood associations, and small businesses.

Expertise at a Glance

Our crew delivers complete building envelopes and interiors:

  • Architectural roofing systems, leak mitigation, and preventative maintenance.
  • Fiber cement, LP SmartSide, and custom siding installations.
  • Exterior remodeling including porches, entries, decks, and outdoor rooms.
  • Interior remodeling across kitchens, baths, trim, and open-concept conversions.
  • General construction support for insurance claims and multi-phase upgrades.
Our Work Philosophy

Every job receives a dedicated lead who coordinates schedules, material deliveries, and daily clean-up. Rutilo personally reviews workmanship milestones to ensure our standards never slip.

Why Choose T&O

Clients appreciate that we balance design insight with field-tested construction knowledge. We provide visuals, samples, and practical recommendations so homeowners can make confident decisions.

Community Support

A portion of seasonal profits funds discounted repairs for neighbors in need and supports local youth trade programs—helping the next generation of builders.
